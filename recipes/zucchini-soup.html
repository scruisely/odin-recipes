Zucchini Soup for Anouk

Home

Home made, delicious and nutrient rich Zucchini Soup.

If you're here because it's cold and raining and you really need a soup to brighten up your day. Then we're here for you.

Got a couple of zucchinis going bad in the bottom of the veg draw? A few old potatoes and some philly cheese? Say no more and follow the recipe below.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbs Olive Oil
  • 1 lrg Onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 4 med Zucchini, chopped
  • 1 ltr (4 cups) vegetable stock
  • 1 tsp dried Thyme (or fresh)
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley or basil for Garnish (optional)
  • 100ml (1/2 cup) cream (optional)
  • Grated Parmesan (optional, for serving)

Method

  1. Saute the Onions and Garlic

    2. Heat the Oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and saute for 5-7 min until softened and translucent.

    Stir in the minced garlic and cook for an additional 1-2 min, until fragrant

  2. Add the Zucchini

    3. Add the chopped Zucchini to the pot, stirring well.

    Cook for approx. 5 min, allowing the Zucchini to soften.

  3. Add Stock and Thyme

    4. Pour in the Vegetable Stock, making sure the Zucchini is fully submerged.

    Add the Thyme and season with the Salt and Pepper to taste.

    Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce heat to low and simmer for 10-15 min, or until Zucchini is tender.

  4. Blend the Soup

    5. Remove the Pot from the heat and use an immersion blender to puree until smooth.

    Less if you prefer a chunkier soup.

    Alternatively, transfer to a blender and blitz in batches.

  5. Add cream (optional) and Adjust Seasoning

    6. For a richer, creamier soup reheat gently and stir in cream. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed.

  6. Serve

    7. Ladle generously into bowls.

    Garnish with chopped Parsley or Basil and (optional) grate Parmesan over your delicous healthy Zucchini Soup.
