Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Home

A quick and easy mid week pleaser. Make this pasta faster than your loved ones can say I'm hungry.

Tired? Just not feeling?. This recipe is for you.

All you're going to need should already be laying around. If you're anything like me.

Ingredients

  • 400g Spaghetti
  • 6 cloves Garlic, finely sliced
  • 1/2 cup EVOO (Extra Virgin Olive Oil)
  • 1 tsp chilli flakes (adjust to taste)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh Parsley, chopped for Garnish
  • Grated Parmesan Cheese for serving (optional)

Method

  1. Cook the Pasta

  2. Saute the Garlic

  3. Combine Pasta and Garlic

  4. Garnish and Serve
